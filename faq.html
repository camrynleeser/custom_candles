When will I recieve my item?

Our orders take approximents 7 business days! If you're having problems tracking your package or your package was lost it's best to reach us on our phone number. We are sorry for any inconvience!

Can I return an item?

Yes! If there is an item that was delivered and you aren't happy with it, we accept returns! Returns have to be done within 30 days from the purchase.

What do I do if my product is out of stock?

If your item is out of stock, we usually have more on the way or close! You can be put on a waiting list to be notified when the item is back in stock.
